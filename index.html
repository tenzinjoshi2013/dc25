Smart Recycling Bin with AI Sorting

A smart recycling bin that uses artificial intelligence to automatically sort waste into appropriate categories (plastic, paper, metal, glass). The bin includes a compaction system to maximize capacity and sensors to notify when it's full. Users earn rewards for proper recycling through a connected app.
