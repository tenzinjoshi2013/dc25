Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about Kidpreneur and our platform.

How can I submit my idea to Kidpreneur?

To submit your idea, simply click on the "Submit Idea" button in the navigation menu. You'll be guided through a step-by-step process to describe your idea, add relevant details, and upload any supporting materials. Once submitted, our team will review it before making it visible to the community.

Is there an age limit to join Kidpreneur?

Kidpreneur is designed for young innovators aged 8-18. We believe that great ideas can come from anyone, regardless of age. If you're outside this age range but still want to participate, you can join as a mentor or supporter.

How can I protect my idea when sharing it?

While we can't guarantee complete protection for ideas shared on our platform, we recommend only sharing enough information to get valuable feedback without revealing your "secret sauce." For more protection, consider discussing your idea with a patent attorney before sharing it widely.

Can I collaborate with other young entrepreneurs?

Absolutely! Collaboration is encouraged on Kidpreneur. You can connect with other young entrepreneurs through our platform, comment on each other's ideas, and even form teams to work on projects together. Check out our "Collaborate" section to find potential teammates.

Are there any fees to use Kidpreneur?

Kidpreneur is completely free for young innovators to use. Our mission is to empower the next generation of entrepreneurs regardless of their financial situation. We're supported by sponsors, grants, and donations to keep the platform accessible to all.
